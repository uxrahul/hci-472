Movie Trivia

Discover fascinating facts about your favorite movies!

Titanic

Did you know that the movie "Titanic" was the first film to gross over $1 billion worldwide? Directed by James Cameron, it held the record for the highest-grossing film for over a decade until it was surpassed by another Cameron film, "Avatar."

Star Wars

The iconic "Star Wars" opening crawl was inspired by the Flash Gordon serials of the 1930s. George Lucas wanted to pay homage to the sci-fi classics that influenced him, and the scrolling text has since become a hallmark of the franchise.

The Godfather

"The Godfather" is widely regarded as one of the greatest films of all time. Marlon Brando, who played Don Vito Corleone, famously stuffed his cheeks with cotton during his audition to create the character's iconic voice.

Jurassic Park

The sounds of the velociraptors in "Jurassic Park" were created using recordings of tortoises mating. The film's groundbreaking use of CGI and animatronics revolutionized the way movies were made.
