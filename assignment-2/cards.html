Principles of Design

Understanding the Core Concepts

Explore the fundamental principles that shape effective design. Each card below delves into a specific principle, offering insights and examples to inspire your creativity!

Unity

Unity in design ensures that all elements work together harmoniously, creating a cohesive and visually appealing composition.

Example of Unity in Design

Painting: 'Composition VIII' by Wassily Kandinsky

Balance

Balance in design refers to the distribution of visual weight, creating a sense of stability and harmony in the composition.

Example of Balance in Design

Artwork: 'Composition with Red, Blue, and Yellow' by Piet Mondrian

Harmony

Harmony in design brings together elements to create a pleasing and consistent composition, ensuring all parts work well together.

Example of Harmony in Design

Artwork: 'Water Lilies' by Claude Monet
